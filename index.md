# Privacy Policy – Pattern Rush

**Last updated:** January 2026

Pattern Rush respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Pattern Rush does **not collect, store, or share any personal data** such as name, email address, phone number, location, or contacts.

Pattern Rush displays ads provided by **Google AdMob**.  
AdMob may collect **anonymous device information** (such as advertising ID) to show ads, in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.

Pattern Rush itself does not have access to or control over this data.

You can learn more about how Google handles data here:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Analytics
Pattern Rush does **not use any analytics tools** and does not track user behavior.

## Children’s Privacy
Pattern Rush does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. Ads are shown in compliance with Google AdMob policies.

## Data Security
Pattern Rush does not store or process personal data on its own servers.
